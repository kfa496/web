Klever Wallet FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What Crypto Accounts can I add to the wallet?

Currently, the wallet supports Bitcoin, Litecoin, DigiByte, Dogecoin, Dash, Ethereum, Tron, Klever, and all tokens from the Tron and Ethereum blockchains.

Can new Coins be added?

Of course! Our team will add new coins constantly. You can suggest the inclusion of a new currency by our support.

Which Tron and ETH tokens are available in the wallet?

You can access any token by searching the app's tokens. But we only selected the main ones for pre-listing.

What is a Klever stable coin?

A stable coin is a cryptocurrency that is priced based on another currency. The advantage of using Klever's stable coins is that they run on Tron's blockchain, a faster blockchain and with lower fees than the original coins (kBTC is a stable coin paired with BTC and kETH is a stable coin paired with ETH).

How to add a token that is not appearing in the wallet?

You just need to follow the steps:

  • 1. Click on the filter button next to the search input on the portfolio screen.
  • 2. Search for the desired Token
  • 3. Activate it to be incorporated into your portfolio. It's just this.
